Build a World-Class "Export Intelligence Hub" Landing Page
Objective

Design and develop a premium, mobile-first, ultra-fast, fully responsive landing page named:

Export Intelligence Hub
Your One-Stop Gateway to India's Complete Export Ecosystem

This landing page should serve as a centralized dashboard containing the 10 most important Indian Government and Export Organization websites, enabling exporters, manufacturers, traders, MSMEs, startups, and students to access every essential export resource from a single location.

The design should feel like a blend of Apple, Google, Stripe, Notion, and India's Digital Public Infrastructure—clean, modern, colorful, lightweight, and highly professional.

Final Goal

Deliver a premium, enterprise-grade, mobile-first "Export Intelligence Hub" that centralizes the 10 most important Indian export websites into a single, intuitive dashboard. The platform should clearly explain the importance, purpose, key features, benefits, and ideal use cases for each portal while allowing users to launch the official websites with one click, making it an indispensable daily resource for every Indian exporter.
